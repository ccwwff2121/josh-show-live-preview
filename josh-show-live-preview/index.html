Meet the Voices Behind the Show

Host & Founder
Lafayette City-Parish President Conservative Leader Louisiana Native

Josh Guillory

Josh Guillory brings a unique perspective as both the Lafayette City-Parish President and a passionate advocate for Louisiana values. With decades of experience in business and public service, Josh combines practical wisdom with principled leadership.

Known for his direct communication style and commitment to transparency, Josh tackles the toughest issues facing Louisiana with common sense solutions rooted in conservative principles. His experience in both the private and public sectors gives him an insider's understanding of how policy decisions impact everyday citizens.

Co-Host
Former State Representative Community Advocate Policy Expert

Bernadette Lee

Bernadette Lee brings sharp political insight and years of legislative experience to every conversation. As a former Louisiana State Representative, she has firsthand knowledge of how laws are made and how they impact communities across the state.

Her expertise in education policy, economic development, and community building makes her an invaluable voice on the show. Bernadette's ability to break down complex policy matters into practical terms helps listeners understand the real-world implications of political decisions.

Co-Host
Attorney at Law Legal Analyst Constitutional Scholar

Robert Judice

Robert Judice provides the legal perspective that grounds the show's discussions in constitutional principles and the rule of law. As an experienced attorney, Robert helps listeners understand the legal implications of current events and policy debates.

His passion for the Constitution and individual rights shines through in every analysis. Robert's ability to explain complex legal concepts in accessible terms makes him an essential part of the show's commitment to educating and empowering its audience.
