Meet Our Hosts

Host & Founder
Mayor-President Army Veteran Professor of Law Lafayette, LA

Josh Guillory

Josh is an intense thinker and cares about contributing positively to the outcome. He is married to his wife, Jamie, and they have three children. An adjunct professor of law, Josh has a passion for our Constitution and American history. Josh was elected as the Lafayette Mayor-President in 2019.

He was blessed to serve approximately 250,000 constituents for four years. In his time in office, he brought conservative principles to local government that led to a balanced budget, a significant increase in investments to infrastructure, a strong economy, the lowest unemployment in the state of Louisiana upon leaving office, and policies that ensured law enforcement and firefighters were adequately paid and had the resources they needed to do their jobs at the highest caliber.

Guillory is also an Army combat veteran. He enlisted in the Louisiana Army National Guard, served on active duty in the Army, and later became a Cavalry officer. Josh served in Baghdad, Iraq in 2005 and is a veteran of Operation Iraqi Freedom. Josh strives to help promote a more civil discussion of policy and politics, standing strong in his values while welcoming a diversity of thought to the conversation.

Jamie Angelle

Jamie Angelle is a true Cajun, born and raised in St. Martin Parish. He spent the past four years at the Lafayette Consolidated Government as Chief Communications Officer and then Deputy Chief of Staff.

He is familiar to Acadiana audiences from his time as the morning anchor/weather forecaster for Allen Media Group's KLAF-TV/KADN-TV from 2017 to 2019 and a reporter for Scripps' KATC-TV in 2019‚Äì2020. Prior to a 13-year career in the United States Army, Angelle was an Assignment Editor at KATC-TV and a reporter for KVHP-TV Lake Charles.

Jamie lives in the Cecilia area with his wife, three daughters, one son, two dogs, and three cats. You can usually find him grilling, cooking, or watching the news in his free time.
